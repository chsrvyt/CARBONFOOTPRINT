# 🌍 TerraFlow — Carbon ESG Command Terminal

> AI-powered sustainability intelligence platform for carbon footprint tracking, ESG analytics, and compliance-ready reporting.

![TerraFlow Banner](assets/banner.png)

## Overview

TerraFlow is a modern ESG and carbon accounting platform designed to help individuals and organizations understand, monitor, and reduce their environmental impact.

The platform transforms complex sustainability data into an intuitive command-center experience, enabling users to track emissions, generate regulatory reports, and gain actionable insights for long-term environmental responsibility.

## Features

### 📊 Carbon Footprint Tracking

Monitor emissions across activities and operational sources through a centralized dashboard.

### 🌱 Sustainability Analytics

Visualize environmental impact trends and track progress toward sustainability goals.

### 📑 ESG Reporting

Generate structured ESG reports aligned with modern sustainability disclosure standards.

### 🔒 Audit-Ready Carbon Ledgers

Maintain transparent and traceable records for compliance and verification purposes.

### 📈 Performance Monitoring

Track key sustainability metrics through real-time dashboards and analytics.

### 🤖 AI-Powered Insights

Receive intelligent recommendations to help reduce emissions and improve ESG performance.

### ⚡ Command Center Interface

Experience sustainability management through a futuristic terminal-inspired dashboard.
